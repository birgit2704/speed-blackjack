Dealer's cards:

RULES

Beat the dealer

How?

By drawing a hand value that is higher than the dealer's value

By drawing a hand value of 21 on your first two cards, when the dealer does not

How do you lose to the dealer?

Your hand value exceeds 21

The dealers hand has a greater value than yours at the end of the round

Special rules

Number cards count their number value

JACK, QUEEN and KING count as 10

An ACE can count as 1 or 11, depending what favours you most

You cannot see the dealer's second card

The dealer will NOT draw an additional card
